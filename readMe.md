# INTRODUCTION

**GRIVAR** is one of those classic text-based RPGs; its story revolves around the disappearance of peasants near a cave. As the King's right-hand man, you (the player)—accompanied by a small contingent of men—were sent to investigate and try to solve the mystery. Upon arriving near the cave, your caravan was attacked by a mysterious force; you barely escaped with your life and sought shelter in an abandoned cabin while recovering from your wounds. Some time has passed since the incident, and you finally wake from your unconscious state—fully restored—only to make a dangerous decision: to explore the area on your own.

# SETUP

1. Open the **Grivar** project on **GitHub** and, on the first screen, click the name **"GRIVAR"** located below **"Releases"**.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/16075612-5b5e-4ce8-8771-10c0b067e4a7" alt="1" width="100%" />

---

2. Once you are on the **releases** screen, click on **grivar-installer.zip**, wait for the download to complete, and then click **"Open folder"** (or **"View files"**) in your browser's download section or locate the folder manually.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/6c80520b-d661-4a9a-bafc-ef8bf92e1ef0" alt="2" width="100%" />

---

3. Left-click on **grivar-installer.zip** and extract it using your file extraction program.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/03bd34f7-6cd9-4e94-87d7-09f9ba43fff2" alt="3" width="350" />

---

4. Once the file is extracted, double-click the file named **GRIVAR.exe** or **GRIVAR_setup.exe**.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/245e29a4-5008-491d-8e4d-d647d32f9790" alt="4" width="350" />

---

5. When the setup file opens, **select the language** you wish to use for the installation.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/e80800c5-1be4-4a68-8a56-26538e260435" alt="5" width="500" />

---

6. Type the path you want to use for the game installation into the text box, or click **Browse** to select it manually.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/c91a96eb-e9b4-456f-a748-e550e4b28784" alt="6" width="700" />

---

7. You can optionally add a **desktop shortcut** by checking (or unchecking) the box.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/a4196ec6-9227-4a52-8f81-b7b97bbf33b3" alt="7" width="700" />

---

8. When everything is ready, click **Install**.
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/f6867560-3530-46c0-bce6-893740d6cfdf" alt="8" width="700" />

---

9. Once the installation is complete, choose whether to launch the game by checking the box, click **Finish**, and enjoy!
   <br><br>
   <img src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/282cdb63-f9c6-4087-8ac3-b44fd352465a" alt="9" width="700" />
